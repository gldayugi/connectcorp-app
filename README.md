ConnectCorp Kenya is a mobile application that helps businesses quickly search, verify, and manage professional corporate contacts. The platform integrates with the Apollo.io API to access a large B2B contact database, allowing users to find professionals by company name, job role, or LinkedIn profile.

The app is designed mainly for SMEs, startups, and sales or marketing teams that need fast, reliable contact information without complex systems. It features a simple mobile-friendly interface with secure login, a powerful search tool, filtered results, and detailed contact cards showing verified professional information.

The goal of the system is to centralize business contact discovery, improve data accuracy, and reduce the time spent on manual research for organizations operating in Kenya and beyond.
